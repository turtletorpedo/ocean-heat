Ocean Heat Content (0–2000 m)

This chart shows how much heat the global ocean has gained over time. Most of the extra heat from climate change is stored in the oceans.

Data file: data/ohc_tidy.csv (format: year,ohc_zj). Generate it with data/fetch_data.sh.
Sources: NOAA NCEI (World Ocean 0–2000 m), Our World in Data (monthly series based on NOAA).
